About Us - West Initiatives

At West Initiatives, we're reshaping the future of real estate financing and construction with a commitment to innovation, integrity, and community. As a young team of entrepreneurs, we bring fresh energy and a forward-thinking approach to every project we take on, from hard money lending to demolition and construction services.

On the Lending side, we specialize in Bridge Loans and DSCR Loans, offering fast, flexible solutions tailored to help businesses thrive in crucial moments. Our financial expertise and client-centric approach ensure that we provide personalized loan services designed to meet unique financial needs quickly and efficiently.

On the Construction side , we handle everything from new builds to demolition, bringing precision, dedication, and quality to every project. Whether it's transforming spaces or creating new ones, our construction services stand out for their attention to detail and commitment to excellence.

At our core, we believe in more than just business success—we believe in building trust, fostering growth, and giving back to the community that supports us. West Initiatives is about more than just transactions; it’s about long-term relationships built on integrity, collaboration, and mutual success.

Fix & Flip

Simply put, a Fix and Flip loan is like a magic wand for real estate investors who seek to buy, repair, and sell a property in a relatively short time. This loan type is the fuel to your real estate transformation engine, allowing you to revamp a property and flip it for profit.

Ground Up

Ground Up loans are the financial foundations for real estate developers looking to start a new construction project from scratch. Think of it as the seed capital that allows your construction dreams to take root and grow.

Multi-family Bridge

Multi-family Bridge loans are your financial stepping stones when investing in multi-unit properties. They provide a short-term financing bridge while you transition to long-term financing, making them perfect for investors looking to capitalize on multi-family real estate opportunities.

Stabilized Bridge

Stabilized Bridge loans are the financial life rafts for investors who have a stabilized property but need short-term capital. They provide the financial stability to bridge the gap until permanent financing can be obtained.

Single Property Rental

Rental Portfolio loans are for the real estate moguls in the making. They offer financing for multiple rental properties, providing the capital you need to build or expand your empire of rental properties.

Family Term

Family Term loans are your financial partners when investing in long-term residential rental properties. Ideal for single-family homes, these loans offer the capital needed to invest in the long-term rental market.
